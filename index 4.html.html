History

IPL was founded in 2008 in India.

In IPL the most succesful team is Mumbai Indians.

In 2022 there was an Mega Auction.
The BCCI announced the format for the upcoming season and the 10 teams have been divided into two groups.

Lalit Kumar Modi (born 29 November 1963) is an Indian businessman and cricket administrator, currently absconded. He was the founder & first chairman and Commissioner of the Indian Premier League.


The first match was played between Kolkata Knight Riders and Royal Challengers Banglore in M Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bangalore at the date of 18 April 2008 and KKR won that match by 140 runs.

The 2008 IPL winner was Rajasthan Royals. The runner up was Chennai Super Kings.

The 2009 IPL winner was Deccan Chargers. The runner up was Royal Challengers Banglore.

The 2010 IPL winner was Chennai Super Kings. The runner up was Mumbai Indians.

The 2011 IPL winner was Chennai Super Kings. The runner up was Royal Challengers Banglore.

The 2012 IPL winner was Kolkata Knight Riders. The runner up was Chennai Super Kings.

The 2013 IPL winner was Mumbai Indians. The runner up was Chennai Super Kings.

The 2014 IPL winner was Kolkata Knight Riders. The runner up was Kings XI Punjab.

The 2015 IPL winner was Mumbai Indians.The runner up was Chennai Super Kings.

The 2016 IPL winner was Sunrisers Heydrabad.The runner up was Royal Challengers Banglore.

The 2017 IPL winner was Mumbai Indians. The runner up was Rising Pune Super Giants.

The 2018 IPL winner was Chennai Super Kings. The runner up was Sunrisers Heydrabad.

The 2019 IPL winner was Mumbai Indians. The runner up was Chennai Super Kings.

The 2020 IPL winner was Mumbai Indians. The runner up was Delhi Capitals.

The 2021 IPL winner was Chennai Super Kings. The runner up was Kolkata Knight Riders.

The 2022 IPL winner was Gujarat Titans. The runner up was Rajasthan Royals.
